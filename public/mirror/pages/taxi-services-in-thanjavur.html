FAQ's

When is the best time to book an airport drop from Thanjavur to Trichy Airport?

It is better to prebook the airport drop at least 12 hours in advance, especially during peak festival seasons, for a reliable, timely service and to avoid last-minute delays. Our drivers are punctual, and they make sure you reach the airport happily and on time.

Are the tolls, parking fees, and temple entry charges included in the fare?

Yes, all obligatory charges like tolls and parking fees, if applicable, are included at the outset. The separate entry fees for temples, if applicable, are payable directly at the site. This billing method makes it clear for all parties involved and allows for no surprises.

Does Pink Taxi operate at night to offer rides to temples?

Certainly, Pink Taxi allows women to visit or go back from the temple at night with well-trained and background-verified female drivers. This grants comfort and assures safety for passengers taking their rides alone.

Is it possible for me to book one cab and use it for different travel spots in one day?

Of course. We offer an hourly hire package that allows you to plan a sightseeing visit in Thanjavur without the need to make multiple bookings, providing you with diverse and cost-efficient flexibility. You can customize your itinerary and enjoy the city at your own pace.

Are small pets allowed in cabs in Thanjavur?

Yes, small pets are admitted with notice in advance. SUVs and Vans are the best pet-friendly travel vehicles, which ensure comfort for both passengers and pets. Our drivers are making sure the journeys are safe and comfortable for everyone.

Will I be able to use UPI or digital wallets for ride payment?

Yes, Red Taxi accepts all digital payments through UPI, wallets, and cards. This goes on to further prove the flexibility and convenience of traveling, especially for a cashless transaction.

What will happen when I want to cancel my booking?

Red Taxi has a refund policy for cancellations. Eligible refunds are processed quickly, depending on timing, and credited to your account. Full details are given in our booking terms and conditions.

Are your drivers acquainted with the routes leading to towns like Kumbakonam or Trichy?

Yes, they are interfaced with all possible major highways as well as the local roads through which one can travel to nearby towns, so that the outstation trips are absolutely comfortable and reliable.

Do you have larger vehicles for family functions or weddings?

Yes, Traveller Vans and Urbania are available for group travel. These vehicles are spacious and comfortable for traveling to weddings, family gatherings, or corporate outings.
