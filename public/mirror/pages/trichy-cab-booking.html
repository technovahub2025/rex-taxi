Thanjavur

Visit the famous Brihadeeswara temple and other legacies of the Tamil culture at Thanjavur in a comfortable ride with Red Taxi. Thanjavur is culturally rich and famous for its heritage. Take a trip to this famous city in Red Taxi’s comfortable cabs and enjoy a joyous holiday.

Kodaikanal

Only a few hours away from Salem, Kodaikanal is a go-to place for enjoying the pleasant climate and visiting tourist destination of interest. Red Taxi provides an inclusive outstation trip package at affordable rates from Trichy – Kodaikanal for you to enjoy a terrific weekend at the hills.

Kumbakonam Temple

Visit the city of the temple in Red Taxi outstation cab. Explore the ancient city in a luxurious ride and go on a spiritual ecstasy with a glorious temple lined across the city. The Outstation cab from Trichy to Kumbakonam provides extensive coverage of tourist spots as per your wish and provides an incredible trip.

Book Now

Mayiladuthurai

Mayiladuthurai gets its name from the Mayuranathaswamy Temple which is a Hindu temple in the town of Mayavaram in Nagapattinam District. The temple is dedicated to Lord Mayuranathaswamy, a form of Shiva, and is very famous in the surroundings. Take a trip to this historic temple in comfort of Red Taxi by a booking an outstation cab from Trichy to Mayiladuthurai.

Book Now

Palani

The famous pilgrim destination, Palani is located on a beautiful hill. Shrine of Lord Muruga is very famous for the main deity that is made from 9 elements with great medicinal and other properties. People throng the temple all around the year for darshan. With Red Taxi hop on and travel from Trichy to Palani in a comfortable at great prices
