FAQ's

How do I book a Red Taxi for the airport transfer from Madurai Airport, and what are the payment options?

You can book cabs from Red Taxi by contacting us through the website, or download the Red Taxi mobile app to have upfront fare estimates, secure cash payment, in-app RedWallet, UPI, or other digital payment methods.

Can I pre-book to arrive late at night or early in the morning at Madurai Airport, and how far in advance can I book it?

Yes, Red Taxi is 24x7 operational for all Madurai airport flights. We serve you from the time they arrive at the airport. You can schedule your pickup well in advance from the mobile app, which is highly recommended for flights.

What kinds of cars are available for airport transfers from Madurai Airport, and how many passengers/bags does each one accommodate?

There are many options, from compact red minis and comfortable sedans (ideal for 4 passengers) to spacious SUVs (up to 7-seaters) capable of carrying larger groups with substantial luggage. Choose the vehicle type according to passenger number and baggage size in the app.

How is the fare calculated for an airport taxi ride, and what other charges does it attract (like toll or parking charges)?

Fares are transparently negotiated by distance, and comprehensive pricing on metered rates includes mandatory Madurai Airport parking or entry charges for the cab and applicable highway tolls.

What are the main safety provisions offered by Red Taxi for a journey from Madurai Airport for a solo traveling woman?

Safety is ensured by real-time GPS tracking of all vehicles and thorough background checks of all drivers. The mobile app provides an in-ride emergency button for human support and allows sharing live trip status with family. We have Pink Taxi, especially ridden by women drivers.

Where is the specific Red Taxi pickup place at Madurai Airport (IXM)?

The specific pickup zone for Red Taxi lies just outside the "Arrival area" of the Madurai Airport terminal. Your driver will call you directly to arrange the appropriate bay so that you can simply walk out of the baggage claim and be picked up easily.

What happens to my prepaid Red Taxi if my flight is delayed? Will it wait for me, and will there be any fees for the waiting period?

If you provide your flight details while booking, the system will keep track of slight delays, but for major changes, you should always get in touch with customer care. There will be a short grace period during which drivers will wait, and after that, minimum waiting charges will apply.

What is the airport booking cancellation policy, and will there be a charge for canceling my ride?

Cancellations can be made on the app or through the call center. Cancellation is free as long as the cancellation is done before a driver gets assigned to you or within a very short period after assignment. A nominal fee may be charged for cancellation if the driver has reached the pickup locality or covered a considerable distance toward the airport.

How can I retrieve a lost item that I may have left in a Red Taxi after my trip from Madurai Airport?

We have 24/7 customer support; immediately contact Madurai Customer Care along with your trip details, like the booking ID and a clear description of the ride. The team will arrange with the driver to locate the item and work out a safe return.

Apart from standard airport drops and pickups, are any other services offered by Red Taxi, such as outstation or hourly rental from Madurai Airport?

Outstation cab services are now available by Red Taxi to shuttle passengers to the respective air terminals and nature-based gateways close to places such as Kodaikanal, Rameswaram, etc. It also has an hourly rental package for certain specified duration rentals basically meant for local sightseeing or for moving across different cities.
