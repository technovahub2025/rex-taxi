Journeying Through Namakkal: Beautiful & Spiritual Places You Simply Cannot Miss.

Kolli Hills

Enveloping the Eastern Ghats, Kolli Hills rise to about 1,200 m, with a stunning green vista, winding hairpin turns, and calming viewpoints. Red Taxi provides Outstation Cabs to herbal farms, botanical gardens, and caves that beckon visitors to awaken their senses at the mystical Agaya Gangai waterfalls deep in the forest.

Book Now

Arapaleeswarar Temple

An ancient Shiva shrine nestled on the Kolli Hills, this temple is intricately carved into the rock and surrounded by intoxicating forest trails. Red Taxi provides tour packages to embrace the gentle sound of the Agaya Gangai waterfall nearby and the serene Siddhar caves, all of which enhance the spiritual atmosphere, which attracts gullible pilgrims and nature lovers.

Book Now

Namakkal Fort & Narasimhaswamy Temple

Towering over the town of Namakkal is a high rock fort cut from a single stone, upon which the rock-cut cave temples of Lord Narasimha and Lord Ranganatha were built. Red Taxi provides an Outstation Cab from Namakkal to the Narasimhaswamy Temple, which is a fine example of stunning Dravidian architecture, especially on the living rock.

Book Now

Namakkal Anjaneyar Temple

At the base of Namagiri Hill, a majestic 18-foot stone statue of Hanuman (Anjaneyar) was carved from a single rock. In addition to the temple's placid atmosphere, it serves as a great symbol of faith and strength by being aligned with the Narasimha shrine. To fully experience this remarkable temple, book our Red Taxi Cabs in Namakkal and explore.
