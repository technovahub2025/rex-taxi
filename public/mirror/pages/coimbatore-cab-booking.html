At Red Taxi, we offer a range of packaged trips to these incredible destinations at competitive prices, ensuring you explore Coimbatore's beauty and culture without compromise. Coimbatore is also a thriving business center with strong industries in textiles, automobile spare parts, spinning mills, and more. Red Taxi is dedicated to serving the needs of business travelers, offering luxury rides designed for optimal comfort and efficiency.

Journey Through Coimbatore: Tourist Spots
You Can't Miss

Ooty

Ooty is the Queen of Hills with scenic beauty and sweeping landscapes that mesmerizes all. Red Taxi provides tour packages to Ooty at affordable rates. Ooty has several tourist spots like the boat house, Dodabetta, Catherine falls, Botanical garden, Sims boathouse and many more. Enjoy visiting these places by booking outstation cabs from Coimbatore to Ooty with Red Taxi

Book Now

Black Thunder

Are you looking for a perfect way to enjoy your summer vacation with your family or friends? Black Thunder is a solid family entertainment spot in Coimbatore. It is a favourite summer destination and vacation spot for families as they take their kids for a day of fun and frolic in the theme park. Red Taxi offers an attractive package to Black Thunder for a fun-filled day out with your family. Book your affordable Taxi in Coimbatore now and enjoy the summer vacation with your kids worry-free.

Book Now

Aliyar Dam

Set is a serene and scenic location Aliyar Dam is the best option for a one-day picnic spot in Coimbatore. Located surrounding Aanamalai Hills with wonder natural views, Aliyar Dam is the perfect spot to visit with your family or friends for a refereshing weekend. Book a cab from Coimbatore to Aliyar Dam at best rates. Red Taxi offers comfortable ides and affordable rates for outstation cabs

Book Now

Athirappilly

Go on a refreshing trip to the Athirappilly falls in the comfort of Red Taxi. Athirappilly is the largest falls in Kerala. A popular tourist spot, Athirappilly is a must-visit for its abundant scenic beauty. Red Taxi allows you to book a cab from Coimbatore to Athirappilly at an affordable price.

Palani

The famous pilgrim destination, Palani is located on a beautiful hill. Shrine of Lord Muruga is very famous for the main deity that is made from 9 elements with great medicinal and other properties. People throng the temple all around the year for darshan. With Red Taxi hop on and travel from Coimbatore to Palani in a comfortable at great prices.

Book Now

Isha

Nestled in the foothills of Velliangiri, Isha Yoga Centre is a peaceful getaway for people of Coimbatore.The massive bust of Adiyogi installed in magnificence is a great attraction of this place. The Dhyanalinga and the Goddess shrines in the Isha Yoga expel peace and serenity. This can be the perfect getaway with the family with a scenic ride and a peaceful destination. Make a weekend much better by booking a cab from Coimbatore to Isha Yoga Centre with Red Taxi.
