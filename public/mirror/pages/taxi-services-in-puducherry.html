FAQ's

Does the fare for the outstation cabs include tolls or taxes?

Yes, because once you've booked your Red Taxi outstation cabs, the fare you get includes tolls and interstate taxes, and applicable road fees in the final amount. Because of this, everything at that point becomes clear regarding pricing with no last-minute surprises.

How is the fare calculated for a one-way drop taxi?

The fare for the one-way drop-off taxi is calculated based only on the distance traveled to your destination. Since it does not charge for the return journey, it is a cost-effective option, most especially for people wishing to travel in one single direction.

What happens when I change my travel plans during travel?

If your travel plans change in the middle of the trip, you could go through the driver or the customer service to request changes in the route or destination. Additional kilometers or minutes will be charged clearly according to the taxi fare, keeping it flexible and without any confusion.

Is Red Taxi open 24/7 for emergencies or midnight trips?

Definitely. Red Taxi operates its services around the clock, making it beneficial for emergencies as well as very early morning departures and late evening arrivals.

Are the drivers familiar with local routes and popular places?

Absolutely. Red Taxi drivers are well-versed with city roads, highways, nearby towns, tourist spots, temples, hospitals, and popular food locations to ensure smooth and time-efficient travel.

What is the cancellation and refund policy for pre-booked taxis?

Red Taxi has an open policy when it comes to cancellation. The eligibility and time frames for refunds are contingent upon the cancellation of a booking. The booking process provides a thorough explanation of the policy.

Can I book an SUV or bigger vehicle for family/group travel?

Yes. From Red Taxi, you can have a wide range of choices of vehicles, such as big SUVs and even Traveller vans, making it a perfect option for family trips, group outings, and lengthy travels with heavy luggage.

Can I track my taxi booking in real time once I confirm it?

Indeed. Upon confirmation of your booking, Red Taxi allows for real-time tracking of taxi services. You would be able to see driver and vehicle information and live location updates to help you plan better for your trip while keeping you informed throughout the journey.

What payment options are accepted for Red Taxi bookings?

The modes that Red Taxi accepts when it comes to booking payment are various, including cash and UPI, as well as popular digital wallets, thus providing customers with their most flexible and easiest payment options.

Can I travel with my pet in a Red Taxi outstation cab?

Pet traveling is allowed according to the size of the pet and the availability of the vehicle. Customers are requested to notify us during booking so that suitable arrangements can be made.
