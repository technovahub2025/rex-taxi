FAQ's

What makes booking an Airport Taxi in Chennai more reliable than using regular local taxis or app cabs?

Chennai's Airport Taxi services provide a more dependable option, as these are primarily for airport patrons who expect timely and professional service. We take charge of proper pickup and drop-off, unlike local taxis or app-based cab services that often cancel at the last moment.

Is it safe to travel from Chennai Airport late at night or early in the morning?

Late at night or in the early hours, do not hamper lives at Chennai Airport, as all activities of pickup, tracking, and driver monitoring go on 24/7. Airport Taxi Services provide drivers who have undergone verification for responsible nighttime travel. The central team monitors the GPS-equipped vehicles.

Are there women-driven Pink Taxis in Chennai, helping women passengers feel more secure?

Yes, Pink Taxis in Chennai are driven entirely by trained women drivers. Generally, these taxis make many women passengers feel safer, especially when they are alone or when arriving late at night. For mothers traveling with kids or young women who are returning from college or work trips, Pink Taxis provide the level of personalized experience and privacy that ordinary taxis cannot.

What next in case my flight gets delayed, and a taxi is already booked?

In case of a flight delay, airport taxi services In the event of a flight delay, the Airport Taxi service automatically adjusts the pickup time because it constantly tracks flight information. In the case of major delays, customer support may non-disruptively assign the nearest available driver to your pickup and travel.

Elderly parents are traveling with me. Will the driver help them move and carry their bags?

Yes, drivers assigned for taxi transfers from Chennai's airport receive training to assist elderly passengers. The drivers do assist with carrying heavy luggage, boarding and alighting from the vehicle, providing a comfortable level of air conditioning in the vehicle, and adjusting their rate of driving to the level of comfort of the passengers.

Can I request a certain type of car, such as a sedan or mini, or SUV, to be assigned for my pickup at Chennai Airport?

Yes, you can specify the exact type of car that you want when making your booking. Taxi services at Chennai Airport offer compact minis for lone travelers, sedans for business or family trips, or SUVs for those with extra luggage or traveling in larger groups.

How does the Airport Taxi in Chennai help provide a smooth experience, especially during traffic hours?

Chennai is notorious for catering to busy roads in peak hours, particularly Guindy, T. Nagar, and OMR. Airport Taxi drivers know the traffic patterns around peak hours and use real-time navigation tools to take the fastest route. Most of the taxi drivers traveling the airport stretch are well acquainted with the alternate entrance and exit routes that help minimize travel time.

Can I request extra assistance if I am travelling with multiple bags or a few items of extravagant luggage?

Yes, if you are travelling with several bags, musical instruments, sports equipment, or oversized suitcases, kindly mention that in advance. The drivers help load the luggage properly to ensure that nothing is damaged in the course of the journey. This approach is mainly helpful for international arrivals who, in most cases, carry extra baggage.

What is the process of recovering something that I accidentally leave in the taxi upon reaching my destination?

You can get in touch with customer care almost immediately if you leave something in the cab. You would be required to provide them with the details of your ride, which includes your booking ID or vehicle number. The Airport Taxi Service in Chennai has a proper driver log-in place, which will help trace your vehicle very easily.

Can I schedule my return trip to Chennai Airport at a specific time on a different day?

Yes, you can book for any future date and time whatsoever for your return trip. This is very beneficial for business travelers, students, senior citizens, and frequent flyers, all of whom like having their travel plans set long in advance. When you schedule one, it reserves a specific driver and vehicle solely for that time period.
