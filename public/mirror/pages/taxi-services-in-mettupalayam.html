FAQ's

How early should I book a cab to Coimbatore Airport from Mettupalayam?

It is advisable to book the cab as early as possible for at least 12 hours to make sure it is set during peak travel seasons. Rest assured that our drivers pick up on time and, most importantly, reach the airport in a stress-free manner.

Are the toll, parking, and ghat road charges included in the fares for trips to Ooty?

Yes, all these mandatory charges, such as tolls and ghat road fees, are included upfront, which means no hidden costs associated with travel.

Does Pink Taxi operate during the late hours of Mettupalayam?

Pink Taxi offers well-trained and thoroughly verified female drivers to ensure safe nighttime travel for women.

Can I also book a cab for more than one sightseeing point on the same day?

Of course, the sightseeing of Mettupalayam and its nearby attractions could be done without rebooking through our hourly rentals clubbing with the unique flexibility they have and cost-exclusive savings.

Do the Mettupalayam cabs accept pets?

Small category pets are allowed with prior notice. Travel in SUVs and vans is suggested for your convenience and the pet's comfort.

Can I pay for my ride with UPI or digital wallets?

Sure, Red Taxi accepts all major digital payment modes, such as UPI, wallets, and cards, which gives flexibility and convenience to customers for cashless transactions.

What should I do if I want to cancel my booking?

Red Taxi has an uncomplicated and very clear cancellation refund policy, wherein reimbursements would be made into your account if eligible quite quickly, depending on the timescale involved.

Do your drivers know the routes to Ooty and Coonoor?

Yes, they are well acquainted with ghat and mountain routes. Your outstation trips become comfortable and reliable, as they drive you safely and efficiently to hill stations.

Will you provide large vehicles for family functions or group outings?

Yes, there are Traveller vans and Urbania available for group traveling. These are spacious and very comfortable, making them perfect for weddings, family gatherings, and corporate outings.

Is Red Taxi available for emergency bookings at night?

Yes, we run service 24/7. Red Taxi can promptly attend to medical emergencies, late-night travel, and urgent airport drops.
