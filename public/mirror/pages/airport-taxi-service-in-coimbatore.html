FAQ's

Which is the best Coimbatore Airport Taxi, and how do I book it?

Red Taxi is recognized as the best Coimbatore Airport Taxi. You can book a Red Taxi by mobile app, website, or over the phone at any time using the customer care number. Usually, app booking is the fastest because you can see the vehicle category, estimated fares, and driver details instantaneously.

Will the driver wait for a delayed flight?

Yes, by adding your flight number while booking, Red Taxi tracks real-time flight status, and, in case of delay, the arrival time of the driver is automatically adjusted. Most drivers will wait for a reasonable time without an extra charge; however, if the delay is longer than expected, additional charges may apply.

How much does an airport taxi generally cost in Coimbatore?

The fares depend on the vehicle type: Nano, Go, Prime, Sedan, and SUV. Base fare with distance fare and minimal charges like airport entry/parking, if applicable, is included in the cost. Red Taxi usually shows the estimated fare before confirming the ride, so it won't be a surprise. Most fares are generally transparent rather than hidden ones.

Where will the taxi pick me up at Coimbatore International Airport?

Red Taxi drivers hold name boards and will assist you with your luggage and pick you up at the airport. They usually contact you via call or text about the exact location and vehicle details after entering the airport premises. The vehicle number and Red Taxi branding will make identification easy.

Can I book a taxi after reaching the airport, or should I book in advance?

Both are possible. Immediate booking goes off best during the usual hours when plenty of taxis are around the area. Booking is necessary during peak hours, late-night arrivals, and festival seasons, and if traveling with a larger group and needing an SUV or Tempo Traveller. Pre-booking guarantees that the driver will be available at the precise time you need.

Do the drivers of the Red Taxis have good knowledge of the routes in Coimbatore?

Yes, almost all the drivers are basically native people and very well versed with the routes of Coimbatore, traffic patterns, and landmark locations like Gandhipuram, RS Puram, Peelamedu, Ukkadam, Avinashi, Pollachi Road, etc. GPS navigation ensures a smooth, fast ride, even for newcomers.

Does Red Taxi assure safety to solo travelers or women traveling at night?

Red Taxi ensures safety and reliability, which makes us the top Cab Service in Coimbatore. Especially for women, we have Pink Taxi to enhance safety and encourage women drivers. All drivers are verified, rides are GPS-trackable, and you can share live ride status with family and friends. Women traveling can choose our Pink Taxi for their professional drivers and efficient services.

What if I have extra luggage or oversized bags?

For extra and oversized luggage, you can choose between a sedan or an SUV during the booking process, depending on your needs. It's beneficial to add information in booking notes or give notice to customer care so that they will give the right vehicle.

Can I request a child seat or travel with kids?

Red Taxi is child-friendly, but child seats are not provided automatically. If you need a baby seat, ask customer service if their cars have one. Otherwise, you will be able to take your child's seat and secure it properly in the car.

What payment options are available for airport rides?

You can pay by cash, UPI, debit/credit cards, or app-based payments, depending on your choice. The bill will be generated within the app after the ride is ended. For corporate customers or frequent flyers, Red Taxi also serves trip invoices for reimbursement.
